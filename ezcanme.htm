Introduction

The website contains the map of CNHS - SHS for everyone to use for their journey inside the school. It fosters a sense of belonging and reduces feelings of being overwhelmed, contributing to a more welcoming and inclusive campus environment. Furthermore, a user-friendly map can improve campus safety by enabling quick access to emergency services and exits. This project aims to enhance the overall campus experience, ensuring a smoother transition for students and guests alike.

Purposes

  • 1. Help freshmen and visitors develop a sense of direction and familiarity with campus through a clear visual layout.
  • 2. Enable easy navigation to key areas like classrooms, offices, and events, with accessible features for all users.
  • 3. Foster social integration by guiding users to gathering spots and student organizations.
  • 4. Create an inviting, safe environment with an intuitive map that improves overall campus experience and access to emergency routes.

Description

  • Clear and Accessible Map
    • The navigation system includes interactive maps with search functions, color-coded paths, and schedule integration, making it easy to locate classrooms and offices.
  • Dedicated Visitor Support
    • Customized maps highlight parking, event spaces, building names/numbers, and main entry points, with accessibility options like high-contrast colors and larger print.
    • Maps are also available at welcome centers with staff assistance.
  • Additional Features for Improved Access
    • The system offers multi-lingual support, a mobile app, and regular feedback collection to keep improving based on user needs.
